## 0:45 - 2:00 | The Science: Why BrainBloom?
**(Visual: Overlay showing the BrainBloom 'Learn' path with Level 1 to 10)**

**Speaker:**
"At the 2025 ASEE Annual Conference, the conversation in engineering education shifted towards 'LLM-Augmented Review Systems'—tools that don't just give you answers but force you to produce them. That is the essence of **Active Recall**. Instead of re-reading a page, BrainBloom forces your brain to retrieve information from memory. This retrieval process strengthens neural pathways, making knowledge stick.

But recall alone isn't enough. You need **Spaced Repetition (SRS)**. Our algorithms are designed to show you information exactly when you’re about to forget it. Not too soon, not too late. Just at the point of maximum cognitive effort. 

In BrainBloom, we’ve gamified this process into a 10-Level journey. You don't just study; you progress through stages of mastery."

## 2:00 - 3:30 | Core Features for Focus
**(Visual: Screencast of the 'Home' page showing Daily Goals and XP Progress)**

**Speaker:**
"Let’s look at how BrainBloom turns theory into practice.

First, **Daily Goals and XP Progression**. Cognitive ability isn't built in a day; it’s built through consistency. Our Daily Goal system ensures you hit that 50 XP threshold every single day, keeping your 'Streak' alive and your momentum high.

Second, the **Custom Course Creator**. Research from Osmosis shows that creating your own challenging questions is one of the best ways to integrate high-level concepts into your curriculum. With BrainBloom, you can create 'Text + Image' courses, engaging both verbal and visual memory centers.

And third, something unique: **Physical Milestones**. High-intensity recall requires high-intensity focus. Between missions, BrainBloom prompts you with grounding exercises—breathing techniques and physical movements—to reset your cognitive load and prevent burnout."
